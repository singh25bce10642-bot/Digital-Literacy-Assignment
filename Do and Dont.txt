Do’s:
• Think before posting; ensure content is respectful and professional.
• Use privacy settings to control who can view your posts.
• Share academic achievements and professional updates to build your digital presence.
• Verify information before reposting to avoid spreading misinformation.
• Engage positively with peers, professors, and professional networks.


Don’ts:
• Post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
• Overshare personal details that could compromise safety.
• Engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
• Share confidential academic or workplace information.
• Rely solely on social media for networking; balance with in-person connections
