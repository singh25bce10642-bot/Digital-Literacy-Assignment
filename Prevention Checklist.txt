Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Verify links before clicking – Always hover over URLs and check the website domain carefully.
2. Never share OTPs or passwords – No bank, app, or institution will ask for them.
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
4. Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and update them regularly.
5. Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions like banking or logging into important accounts.
6. Check sender details carefully – Look for spelling errors or unusual email addresses.
7. Keep your apps and devices updated to fix security vulnerabilities.
8. Limit personal information shared online to reduce risk of identity theft.

UPI / Financial Safety Tips:

1. Always verify the recipient’s name before approving UPI payments.
2. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money – it is only required to send money.
3. Do not approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” from strangers.

Reporting & Help:

Report cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent assistance

Staying alert and cautious can significantly reduce the risk of falling victim to cybercrime
